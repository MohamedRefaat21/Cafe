Katameya Clinic Hospital (KCH)

Katameya Clinic Hospital (KCH) (An MHP Hospital) was established in 2019 and is one of the largest and most prominent private hospitals in New Cairo. The hospital has 170 beds and has a total built-up area of 14,00 sqm. Phase one is currently operational and has 85 beds on a 9,000 sqm built-up area. MENA Health Partners (“MHP”) aims to construct and operate Egyptian healthcare hospitals so as to create an interconnected network of hospitals in order to tap the increasing demand for quality healthcare. KCH is the only fully equipped hospital in First Settlement. KCH is conveniently located near the Suez Road and the Ring Road. It is only 15 minutes away from Cairo International Airport, Make it an easy destination to reach local and international patients.
